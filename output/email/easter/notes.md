## Key Decisions Made

- **Subject line changed** from question ("Was wäre, wenn Ostern schon bereit ist?") to statement ("Es riecht nach Ostern hier") — brand guidelines forbid questions in subject lines
- **Opening line cut** — "Was wäre, wenn Ostern schon bereit ist?" was the original subject line repurposed as body opener; removed as redundant once subject changed
- **Snow opener added** — it was snowing in Wattens on send day; "Der Frühling ist da" replaced with "Draußen schneit es noch. / Und trotzdem: Ostern ist in 6 Tagen." for authenticity
- **Wachspasten link added** — email mentioned Wachspasten in prose but original had no link to them; added as fourth → link
- **Holz-Eier / Styropor-Eier added** — email described egg decoration but didn't surface the eggs themselves as products; added inline with hyperlinks to search page
- **Discount extended to Easter** — original had "bis 31. März" which coincided with logistics cutoff; extended to "bis Ostern" (5 April) to keep discount window open for post-Easter orders
- **Phone number corrected** — original brief had wrong number (+43 5224 22599); correct number is 0664 4564271 (Tel./WhatsApp)
