## Edited vs. Original Files

### What Are "-EDITED" Files?

Files with `-EDITED.md` suffix have been processed through the **Seven Sweeps Copy-Editing Framework**:

1. **Sweep 1: Clarity** — Every sentence immediately understandable
2. **Sweep 2: Voice & Tone** — Consistent throughout
3. **Sweep 3: So What** — Benefits clear, outcomes stated
4. **Sweep 4: Prove It** — Claims supported or softened
5. **Sweep 5: Specificity** — Concrete numbers, times, quantities
6. **Sweep 6: Heightened Emotion** — Emotional texture without manipulation
7. **Sweep 7: Zero Risk** — All barriers removed

### Which Version to Use?

**Use EDITED versions for:**
- Main campaign emails (Email 2) — most complex, most impact
- High-stakes sends
- When you want maximum conversion optimization

**Use ORIGINAL versions for:**
- Quick deployment
- When edited versions feel over-optimized
- Testing different intensities

**Both versions:**
- Follow Bastelschachtel brand voice
- Avoid forbidden phrases
- Use soft CTAs
- Include permission narrative

## Campaign Philosophy Reminder

From the [Campaign Summary](CAMPAIGN-SUMMARY-Mothers-Fathers-Day-2026.md):

> **"We don't create desire to craft — we release desire that already exists. Every email removes a barrier, never installs a motivation."**

These campaigns are **permission moments**, not sales deadlines.

The customer already wants to give something meaningful.  
Our job: Open the door. Clear the path. Offer help.

Not push. Not pressure. Not guilt.  
Just: "Here's how. Here's what you need. We'll help you."
