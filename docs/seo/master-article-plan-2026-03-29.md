# Master Article Plan

Date: 2026-03-29

Purpose:
- Define the next SEO content layer after the priority category intros
- Turn the strongest commercial categories into hub-style master articles
- Create a clear linking structure between informational content and money pages

## Why This Is The Right Next Step

The category intros are now strong enough to anchor the commercial pages.

The bigger SEO opportunity is to build master articles that:
- target informational and mixed intent
- establish topical authority around the specialist categories
- link readers into the matching category pages
- give the blog a clearer commercial support role

This is better than spending another round polishing category intros in isolation.

## What A Master Article Should Do

Each master article should act as:
- a deep introductory guide for the topic
- a search-friendly hub for supporting subtopics
- a bridge into category pages, products, tutorials, and project content

Each one should help answer:
- what this category is
- who it is for
- what materials or choices matter
- common beginner mistakes
- how to start
- where to shop the right products on Bastelschachtel

## Suggested Internal Linking Model

Each master article should link:
- down into the matching category or landing page
- sideways into 2-4 relevant tutorials or narrower posts
- into selected products or product groupings only where genuinely useful

Each matching category page should link back to:
- its master article
- 1-3 supporting tutorials

This creates a cleaner structure:
- master article = informational hub
- category page = commercial hub
- tutorials = subtopic depth

## Article Format Recommendation

Each master article should use a repeatable structure:

1. Clear H1 with search intent
2. Short intro that defines the category and who it is for
3. What it is / why people use it
4. What you need to get started
5. Common mistakes or confusion points
6. How to choose the right materials
7. Project or use-case ideas
8. FAQ section
9. Internal links to category page and relevant products
