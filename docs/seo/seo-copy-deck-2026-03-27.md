## How This Deck Is Structured

This deck follows the actual site hierarchy and page roles.

Priority order:
1. Homepage
2. Blog index
3. Core commercial landing page: `/pages/bastelbedarf`
4. Specialist category pages in the current menu:
   - Pentart Shop
   - Reispapier
   - Bastelsets
5. Specialist search landing pages to create or strengthen:
   - Korbflechten / Peddigrohr
   - Silikonformen für Gips & Beton

For each page, this deck includes:
- Page role
- SEO title
- Meta description
- H1
- Supporting headline / intro copy
- Body copy sections
- FAQ prompts
- Internal-link targets

## Site-Architecture Notes

Current top-level structure relevant to SEO:

```text
Homepage (/)
├── Bastelsets & Bundles
│   ├── Für Weihnachten
│   ├── Die Bastelschachtel Ecke
│   ├── Zum Verschenken
│   ├── Für zu Hause
│   └── Mit Kindern
├── DIY
│   ├── Glasmalerei & Ätzung
│   ├── Farben & Veredelung
│   ├── Papier & Decoupage
│   ├── Korbflechten
│   ├── Material & Formen
│   ├── Zubehör und Werkzeuge
│   ├── Bastelmaterial
│   └── Saisonale Deko
├── Blog
└── Katalog
```

SEO implication:
- The homepage must introduce the broad specialist authority.
- `/pages/bastelbedarf` should act as the broad commercial landing page.
- Specialist pages should sit one level below the broad commercial layer and link laterally where relevant.
- Blog posts should support these money pages through internal links.
- `Bastelsets & Bundles` is not just a generic set bucket. Based on the current menu and collection makeup, it should strongly connect to gifting, easier starts, and especially Peddigrohr / Flechtsets.
