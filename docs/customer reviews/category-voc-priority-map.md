## Purpose

Use the actual shop/category structure as the master list.

Then prioritize categories based on:
- business importance
- SEO importance
- strength of available VOC
- ease of turning that VOC into useful category copy

This is better than letting the review set define the taxonomy on its own.

## Master Category Structure

Based on the current strategy and SEO docs, the clearest working category set is:

1. Pentart
2. Reispapier / Decoupage
3. Korbflechten / Peddigrohr
4. Glasätzung
5. Silikonformen für Gips & Beton
6. Bastelsets
7. Metallic & Patina / finishing products
8. Papier & weitere Dekomaterialien
9. Zubehör und Werkzeuge

Notes:
- `Bastelsets` is a merchandising and conversion category, not just a material family.
- `Glasätzung` is strategically important enough to treat as its own specialist category even though it also sits inside the Pentart/product world.
- `Metallic & Patina` likely overlaps heavily with Pentart, but it is commercially important enough to keep separate for navigation and landing-page logic.

## Recommended Writing Order

### Tier 1: Write First

These have the best combination of commercial importance and usable VOC.

1. Pentart
2. Reispapier / Decoupage
3. Glasätzung
4. Silikonformen für Gips & Beton

### Tier 2: Write Next

These matter commercially, but the copy needs more structural reasoning and less raw review language.

5. Korbflechten / Peddigrohr
6. Bastelsets

### Tier 3: Expand Later

These are better handled after the core commercial pages are sharper.

7. Metallic & Patina / finishing
8. Papier & weitere Dekomaterialien

## Category-by-Category Read

## 1. Pentart

Why it belongs near the top:
- Clear business importance in strategy docs
- Strong SEO importance in current page plans
- Rich VOC already available
- Strong overlap with visible bestsellers and repeat-use language

What customers give you:
- trust in the brand line
- finish and effect language
- ease-of-use proof
- repeat-buyer confidence

What to write:
- category intro
- collection H1 and meta
- sub-family copy later for wax pastes, structure pastes, chalky, pigments, effect media

## 2. Reispapier / Decoupage

Why it belongs near the top:
- named as a core category in strategy docs
- already a priority collection in SEO work
- strong VOC around motifs, quality, and decorative outcomes

What customers give you:
- motif-led appeal
- quality language
- real-life beauty versus online photos
- decoupage and decorative use cases

What to write:
- category intro
- collection metadata
- later support content for beginner decoupage and motif-led landing pages

## 3. Korbflechten / Peddigrohr

Why it belongs near the top:
- clearly high-value in business context
- major SEO gap and landing-page opportunity
- strong intent keywords exist

Constraint:
- current VOC set is weaker than for Pentart, Reispapier, and Glasätzung

What to write:
- category page based more on business logic, intent, and category understanding
- later enrich with customer calls, support logs, product reviews, or bundle feedback

Important:
- do not wait for perfect VOC before building this page
- this is one of the clearest commercial-intent opportunities

## 4. Glasätzung

Why it belongs near the top:
- top seller in strategy docs
- clear search and purchase intent
- very strong outcome-led VOC already exists

What customers give you:
- first-use confidence
- beginner friendliness
- visible result
- gift and personalization use cases

What to write:
- category intro
- FAQ/help copy
- tutorial-linked support content

## 5. Silikonformen für Gips & Beton

Why it belongs near the top:
- called out as an important growth/depth category
- already identified as a specialist landing-page opportunity
- enough VOC exists to support precision/detail messaging

Constraint:
- sample size is smaller than the strongest families

What to write:
- category intro
- simple proof-oriented copy
- later expand with bundle logic and project-recipe content

## 6. Bastelsets

Why it matters:
- high conversion potential
- important for gifts, easier starts, and merchandising
- already central in SEO copy deck

Constraint:
- raw VOC is not strong as a standalone material family

What to write:
- copy from merchandising logic
- beginner convenience
- gifting
- easier starts
- curated combinations

This page should not wait on review mining.

## 7. Metallic & Patina / Finishing

Why it matters:
- named as a growing category
- visible overlap with strong Pentart review language

Constraint:
- it currently borrows much of its proof from Pentart sub-families rather than standing alone

What to write:
- probably better as a Pentart-led sub-family page first
- later can become a broader finishing/effects landing page

## 8. Papier & weitere Dekomaterialien

Why it is later:
- broader and fuzzier category
- weaker concentrated VOC
- lower strategic clarity than the top specialist categories

What to do:
- keep it in navigation
- do not prioritize copy-heavy landing-page work yet

## 9. Zubehör und Werkzeuge

Why it is later:
- highly functional category
- low distinctive VOC
- weaker strategic leverage for brand differentiation

What to do:
- keep copy short and utilitarian
- prioritize internal linking and merchandising over long-form category copy

## Best Practical Sequence

If the goal is to improve the shop quickly without overcomplicating the taxonomy, do this:

1. Write category intros for:
   - Pentart
   - Reispapier / Decoupage
   - Glasätzung
   - Silikonformen für Gips & Beton
2. Write strategic pages from category logic for:
   - Korbflechten / Peddigrohr
   - Bastelsets
3. Expand into sub-family and support pages for:
   - Metallic & Patina
   - Pentart effect families
   - tutorial-linked pages

## Bottom Line

The right approach is not `only write the categories with the most review mentions`.

The right approach is:
- use the real shop/category structure as the backbone
- use VOC where it is strongest
- do not delay strategically important categories like `Korbflechten / Peddigrohr` or `Bastelsets` just because the review set is thinner there
- treat VOC as a force multiplier, not as the sole source of truth
