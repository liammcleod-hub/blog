## How To Use This

This bank is organized by product family rather than channel.

- Use it for category-page copy, product-page messaging, merchandising text, email angles, and paid/social hooks.
- Pair it with `docs/customer reviews/voc-split-bank.md` when you need to distinguish between marketplace friction language and owned-channel outcome language.
- Treat repeated wording as directional VOC, not as a statistically clean frequency model.

## Pentart

### What Customers Praise

- Strong product performance and reliable results
- Easy handling and pleasant working texture
- Visible finish effects such as metallic shine, vintage effects, texture, and coverage
- Breadth of use across surfaces and techniques
- Repeat-buyer confidence in the Pentart line overall

### Voice Of Customer

- "Die Pentart Farben sind sehr gut."
- "Ich benutze nur noch Produkte von Pentart."
- "super Farbe, lässt sich gut und leicht verarbeiten, klasse Ergebnis"
- "leicht zu streichen, gute Deckkraft und schauen super aus"
- "hält was der Name verspricht. Toller Effekt."
- "beste metallischen Farben, die ich kenn"
- "Die Einsatzmöglichkeiten sind nahezu unbegrenzt."
- "Beste Strukturpaste die ich je hatte!"

### What This Means

- Pentart is not experienced as a generic brand. It is experienced as a trusted specialist line.
- The strongest conversion language is outcome-led: effect, finish, coverage, texture, ease of use.
- Customers often talk like returning users who already know the line and want more colors, more effects, or more ways to use it.

### Messaging Angles

- Pentart für starke Effekte und saubere Ergebnisse
- Leicht zu verarbeiten, stark im Finish
- Für Mixed Media, Schablonen, Vintage-Effekte und detailreiche Oberflächen
- Farben und Pasten, die halten, was sie versprechen

### Cautions

- Most Pentart feedback in the owned channel is highly positive and technique-oriented, so it can overstate enthusiast confidence versus first-time buyers.
- If writing for beginners, translate expert enthusiasm into simpler proof points like `leicht aufzutragen`, `gute Deckkraft`, and `funktioniert auch beim ersten Versuch`.

## Reispapier

### What Customers Praise

- Attractive motifs and emotional appeal of the designs
- Good material quality
- Good fit for decoupage and decorative projects
- Quick delivery and safe arrival when packaging works
- Specialist assortment and discovery value

### Voice Of Customer

- "Das Reispapier kam sicher verpackt und schnell bei mir an."
- "Sehr schöne Qualität."
- "Oh wunderschönes Reispapier"
- "Werde ab jetzt immer hier bestellen."
- "Die verschiedenen Motive sehen in Natur noch besser aus als Online abgebildet"
- "tolles design"
- "tolles ergebnis mit der serviettentechnik"
- "Ich bin vom gesamten Angebot an Reispapier sehr begeistert"

### What This Means

- Reispapier buyers respond to motif quality first, then material quality.
- The product is often judged by how beautiful it looks in real life and how easy it is to turn into a finished decorative result.
- Assortment matters: customers value finding unusual or nostalgic motifs they cannot easily get elsewhere.

### Messaging Angles

- Reispapier mit Motiven, die in echt noch schöner wirken
- Schönes Design trifft gute Qualität
- Für Decoupage, Serviettentechnik und dekorative Projekte mit sichtbarem Ergebnis
- Große Auswahl für alle, die das besondere Motiv suchen

### Cautions

- Packaging matters more here than for many other families because format damage is visible immediately.
- One recurring pain point is folded A3 sheets: "Leider wird das A3-Format geknickt und nicht gerollt verschickt". If you sell larger formats, expectation-setting should be explicit.

## Glasätzpaste

### What Customers Praise

- Works on the first try
- Easy application and beginner-friendly use
- Strong visual transformation for gifts, glasses, mirrors, and decorative objects
- Creamy consistency and good spreadability
- Good documentation and safety guidance when included

### Voice Of Customer

- "Hat gleich beim ersten Versuch geklappt"
- "Voll super die Paste."
- "Funktioniert super."
- "cremig, sie lässt sich also sehr gut auftragen"
- "Glasätzpaste funktioniert wunderbar"
- "Anwendung total einfach und gut beschrieben"
- "super leicht in der Anwendung"
- "Das Ergebnis ist top."

### What This Means

- The dominant promise is confidence: customers want to know it will work, even if they have never etched glass before.
- Ease and result quality matter more than technical specs.
- This family is especially strong for gift-making, personalization, and satisfying first-use success.

### Messaging Angles

- Glasätzpaste, die auch beim ersten Versuch überzeugt
- Einfach aufzutragen, stark im Ergebnis
- Für Gläser, Spiegel und kreative Geschenke mit persönlichem Finish
- Cremige Paste für saubere, gleichmäßige Ätzung

### Usage Notes From VOC

- Customers repeatedly mention that a thick, even layer improves the result.
- Some recommend waiting longer than 15 minutes for a stronger effect.
- Safety and prep information are valued, especially for new users.

### Cautions

- Some positive reviews include technique corrections that should be surfaced proactively in product copy or FAQs.
- This is a product where better instructions can directly reduce disappointment.

## Silikonformen / Gießformen

### What Customers Praise

- Fine detail and clean reproduction
- High perceived quality
- Versatility across themes and decorative uses
- Strong fit for resin and detailed casting work
- Safe packaging and reliable delivery reinforce trust

### Voice Of Customer

- "sehr detailreiche Siliconform"
- "filigrane und vor allem detailreiche Gießformen"
- "sehr hochwertig"
- "Diese Siliconform kann ich jedem empfehlen"
- "macht detailgenaue Abgüsse"
- "Produkte sicher verpackt"

### What This Means

- Buyers care about precision. They want to know the mold captures detail cleanly.
- The product family is strongest when presented as a tool for crisp, decorative results rather than as a generic mold.
- The surrounding material system matters: customers talk about what resin or powder works best, not just the form itself.

### Messaging Angles

- Silikonformen für filigrane, detailreiche Abgüsse
- Hochwertige Formen für kreative Gießprojekte mit klarer Struktur
- Ideal für Resin, Keramikpulver und dekorative Oberflächen
- Saubere Details statt verwaschener Formen

### Usage Notes From VOC

- Customers discussing advanced casting often reference PU resin and material pairing.
- This suggests upsell opportunities: molds plus resin plus finishing products.

### Cautions

- The sample size is smaller than for Pentart or Reispapier, so keep claims tighter.
- The family likely benefits from stronger education and bundle merchandising more than from broad emotional copy.

## Cross-Family Read

### Strongest Repeating Proof Points

- Easy to work with
- Strong visible result
- Specialist quality
- Good assortment
- Fast delivery when operations go right

### Best Reusable Language

- `leicht zu verarbeiten`
- `gute Deckkraft`
- `toller Effekt`
- `sehr detailreich`
- `sehr hochwertige Qualität`
- `funktioniert wunderbar`
- `tolles Ergebnis`
- `schnell geliefert`

### Messaging Implications

- Pentart should be framed around trusted specialist performance and effect-rich finishes.
- Reispapier should be framed around motif beauty, quality, and decorative transformation.
- Glasätzpaste should be framed around first-use confidence and satisfying visible results.
- Silikonformen should be framed around detail fidelity and high-quality casting outcomes.
