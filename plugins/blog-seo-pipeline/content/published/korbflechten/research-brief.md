### Content Gaps (Bastelschachtel Opportunity)
1. **Missing**: Psychological angle for suppressed creatives
   - Competitors don't address the hesitation/fear
   - Bastelschachtel can: "You already want to create. Here's permission + path."

2. **Missing**: Hand-packed quality differentiation
   - Competitors treat all suppliers equally
   - Bastelschachtel can: "4-person team, carefully selected, hand-packed."

3. **Missing**: Community/permission messaging
   - Competitors are how-to focused only
   - Bastelschachtel can: "This is for you. Your creative time matters."

4. **Missing**: Connection to Bastelschachtel brand story
   - Generic craft supply angles
   - Bastelschachtel can: Authentic Austrian craft heritage, quality promise

### Differentiation Strategy

**Not this**:
"Here are the 7 materials you need to buy for basket weaving. Click here to shop our catalog."

**But this**:
"You want to create something beautiful. Basket weaving is simpler than you think. Here's what you actually need. Here's how to start small."
