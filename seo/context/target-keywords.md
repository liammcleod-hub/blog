**Positioning:** Austrian specialist craft supplier with deep expertise across 5 core categories

## Keyword Research Methodology

- Google Search Console (Bastelschachtel's actual traffic)

**Research Template for New Topics:**
1. Identify primary keyword (commercial intent, Austrian/German variant)
2. Google "people also ask" — becomes supporting article topics
3. Check top 3 competitors' structure — map article outline
4. Identify secondary keywords (variations, "for beginners", troubleshooting)
5. Plan 3-5 supporting articles + money page link targets

## German Language Specifics

**Note:** All keywords above are in German (de-DE), optimized for Austrian market intent.

**Key conventions:**
- `[noun] kaufen` = buying intent (high commercial)
- `[noun] für Anfänger` = beginner-friendly intent
- `[noun] Anleitung` = step-by-step how-to intent
- `[noun] Österreich` = Austrian origin/supply signal
- Capitalize nouns (German grammar standard)
