# Bastelschachtel — Product Categories & Value Propositions

This document outlines Bastelschachtel's key product categories, benefits, and differentiators to inform content creation that drives conversions and customer acquisition.

## Product Categories & Value Propositions

### 1. **Pentart — Farben, Pasten & Medien**
- **What it is**: Complete system for decorative painting, glazing, and effects on wood, glass, mixed media
- **Who uses it**: Crafters doing surface decoration, glass painting, mixed media, furniture refinishing
- **Key benefit**: Creates professional-quality finishes and special effects without complexity
- **Content angle**: "Pentart for [technique]: Step-by-step tutorials, project ideas, material combinations"
- **Internal link target**: `/collections/ihr-pentart-lieferant` and Pentart landing page

### 2. **Reispapier & Decoupage**
- **What it is**: Fine decorative papers with thematic prints for decoupage, collage, surface design
- **Who uses it**: DIY home decorators, gift-makers, furniture upcyclers, mixed media artists
- **Key benefit**: Adds artistic detail and personality to otherwise plain objects; beginner-friendly
- **Content angle**: "Decoupage projects for [object type]: How to choose, apply, seal"
- **Internal link target**: `/collections/reispapier` and Decoupage tutorials

### 3. **Korbflechten — Peddigrohr & Flechtsets**
- **What it is**: Natural and prepared reed materials for basket weaving, structured handcraft projects
- **Who uses it**: Experienced crafters, structural makers, gift-givers wanting handmade objects
- **Key benefit**: Satisfying handcraft that produces heirloom-quality objects; meditative process
- **Content angle**: "Basket weaving for beginners: Material types, first projects, where to start"
- **Internal link target**: Korbflechten landing page and basket-weaving tutorials

### 4. **Silikonformen für Gips & Beton**
- **What it is**: Reusable silicone molds for decorative casting projects in plaster and concrete
- **Who uses it**: Home decorators, gift-makers, beginners trying casting for the first time
- **Key benefit**: Turn casting into accessible, repeatable projects; produces professional-looking results
- **Content angle**: "Gips/Beton casting ideas: Mold selection, pouring, finishing, decoration ideas"
- **Internal link target**: Casting tutorial blog posts

### 5. **Bastelsets — Ready-to-Start Bundles**
- **What it is**: Pre-curated kits combining all materials needed for complete projects (esp. Peddigrohr sets)
- **Who uses it**: Beginners, gift-givers, busy makers who want to skip material selection
- **Key benefit**: Eliminates decision paralysis; gives clear starting point; reduces friction
- **Content angle**: "Which Bastelset for your skill level: Beginner sets, gift sets, family crafting"
- **Internal link target**: Bastelsets category and seasonal bundle pages

### 6. **Glasätzung & Glasmalerei**
- **What it is**: Glass etching paste and glass paints for decorating, personalizing glass objects
- **Who uses it**: Home decorators, gift-makers, people personalizing drinkware or windows
- **Key benefit**: Professional-looking personalization and decoration of glass; durable results
- **Content angle**: "Glass etching for [project type]: Safety, technique, design ideas"
- **Internal link target**: Glass etching tutorials and material pages

### 7. **Multi-Category Gift-Making**
- **What it is**: The intersection of Bastelschachtel materials for handmade gift creation
- **Who uses it**: The Suppressed Creative making gifts for loved ones
- **Key benefit**: Gives permission and tools for meaningful, handmade gift-giving
- **Content angle**: "DIY gift ideas using [materials]: Skill level, time, personalization tips"
- **Internal link target**: Relevant material collections and gift-focused landing pages

## Competitive Differentiators

### vs. Amazon & eBay (Large Marketplaces)
- **Personal curation** (not algorithmic selection)
- **Expert guidance** (tutorials, not just product listings)
- **Austrian/local origin** (trusted, not faceless)
- **Small-team authenticity** (real people, hand-packed)

### vs. Specialty Single-Category Competitors
- **Multi-specialist breadth** (Pentart + Reispapier + Korbflechten all equally strong)
- **Complete solution** (materials + guidance + permission)
- **Unified shopping experience** (one trusted source for multiple crafts)

### vs. Pinterest/YouTube (Content-Only)
- **Buy-ready** (inspiration + materials in one place)
- **Curated selection** (not overwhelming)
- **Quality assurance** (materials tested, projects verified)

## Customer Segments & Needs

### The Suppressed Creative
- **Who**: Busy professionals, parents who loved making as kids but stopped
- **Need**: Permission, gentle re-entry, low-complexity projects, gift-giving
- **Content angle**: "Take back your creative time", "gift-making permission"
- **Products**: Bastelsets, Reispapier, Pentart

### The Experienced Maker
- **Who**: Long-time crafters, hobby enthusiasts, advanced skill level
- **Need**: New techniques, specialty materials, challenge, mastery
- **Content angle**: Advanced tutorials, technique deep-dives, material science
- **Products**: All categories, emphasis on Korbflechten, specialty Pentart

### The Gift-Giver
- **Who**: People buying gifts, parents, partners wanting meaningful gifts
- **Need**: Easy projects, finished-looking results, time-appropriate, emotional value
- **Content angle**: "Gift ideas for busy people", "handmade vs. store-bought"
- **Products**: Bastelsets, gift bundles, Reispapier, Gips forms

### The Beginner
- **Who**: Trying crafting for the first time, low confidence, low technical knowledge
- **Need**: Reassurance, clear steps, fool-proof projects, materials that work
- **Content angle**: "[Craft] für Anfänger", "This is easier than you think"
- **Products**: Bastelsets, tutorials, Reispapier, Pentart basics
